Our mission

Wonder exists all around the British Isles, and offers an affordable way to make new memories and have great experiences without the costs and ongoing problems with international travel. Join use, as we help you get safely adventure outwards, To the Great Outdoors!


Features

We help you find the right location for your day out. Be it a hike through the wilderness to a picnic in a park, we can help you find whats best suited for you. Don't just take our word for it, as each location has a user review system where others (and you!), can leave vital feedback. Did a location promise wheel chair access but failed to deliver, or did they go out their way to make you feel welcome? Let us know, so we can all enjoy the outdoors, regardless of personal situations.
